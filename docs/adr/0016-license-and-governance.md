# ADR-016: License & governance

> Status: Accepted · Frozen in Architecture v1.0 (2026-07-12) · Source: [Doc 15](../architecture/15-adrs.md)

**Context:** Unresolved item #6. **Decision:** **Apache-2.0**, DCO sign-off (no CLA), maintainer-led governance documented in GOVERNANCE.md at Phase 11; two-maintainer agreement required for eval-corpus ground-truth labels (review-adopted process) and for any change to the Engineering Constitution (Doc 22). **Alternatives:** MIT (acceptable, weaker patent posture for enterprise adoption); MPL/AGPL (kills agent-host bundling). **Consequences:** enterprise-compatible from day one; foundation transfer remains possible later (Apache-2.0 + DCO is the compatible posture).
